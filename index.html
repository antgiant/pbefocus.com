Built for PBE Families and Teams, Shared with Gratitude

Like many families before us, when our kids first became interested in Pathfinder Bible Experience, also known as Bible Bowl or PBE, we had no idea what we were stepping into: countless hours of memorization, deep study across the Bible, and all the tips, tricks, and quirks that make PBE its own world.

Along the way we found an amazing community of families, coaches, and students doing the same work, often connecting only briefly at competitions. After years of learning, studying, and building tools to smooth out a few rough edges, we wanted to give something back. PBEfocus gathers the resources we know about, or have created, as a free gift to the PBE community. We pray these tools are useful to your family and your team.

PBE Score Keeper

Scoring your practice sessions made easy

  • Designed for your phone, works great on a computer too.
  • Configure teams, blocks/groups, question timers, and more
  • Fast and easy score entry with advanced options like extra-credit
  • Real time score tracking by team, by block/group and more. No more tedious score calculations
  • Want to do advanced analytics? We have that covered too with all the export options you can dream of
  • All data is yours, it lives on your device not some server
  • Use it for practice, or maybe even your next competition

Fill-In-The-Blank Question Creator

PBE Study Tool for blanked-verse practice

  • Advanced algorithms to blank the tricky words not the easy ones
  • Choose chapters or even individual verses
  • Quick and easy presets for repeat study sessions
  • Set min/max blanks and maximum blank percentage
  • Don't want variation, lock in the exact percentage you want blanked
  • Reveal one blank at a time or all at once
  • Optionally enjoy some rouge sheep bringing some extra joy to your study sessions
